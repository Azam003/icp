About

The ICP Degen Gameverse is algorithmically generated 10,000 Apes living on ICP Blockchain. IDG is a community-driven gambling ecosystem, which is fully decentralized. Each Ape holder will have a stake in the project and will be able to decide the future strategy of the project development.
